Information We Collect

The directivecraft.com website does not collect, store, or process any personal information about visitors. There are no analytics tools, tracking pixels, session recording scripts, or advertising networks on this site.

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log standard server-side request data (IP address, browser type, pages requested, timestamps) as part of normal hosting infrastructure. This data is subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement and is not accessible to or controlled by Directive Craft LLC.

Email Contact

If you contact us by email, we receive and read your message. We use that information only to respond to you. We do not add you to mailing lists, share your address with third parties, or use your contact for any purpose beyond the conversation you initiated.

Our email uses privacy-focused forwarding from our domain address to a private inbox. No message content is stored on intermediate servers beyond standard delivery processing.

Fonts and External Resources

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts. This request is made directly from your browser to Google's servers and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. No other external resources are loaded.

Cookies and Local Storage
